The Norcross Wildlife Sanctuary and Founation: Who we are

The Norcross Wildlife Sanctuary’s website’s purpose is provide those interested in wildlife sanctuaries and conservation, with a website to learn about the services the Sanctuary offers free of charge. The goal of the new website will be for visitors to get more out of their visits to the sanctuary by way of having more information about what is inside the sanctuary itself and how to identify different plants and animals in the area. An additional goal is to provide those community environmental groups seeking small funding for projects to apply to the Norcross Foundation for grants.
